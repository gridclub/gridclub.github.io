Welcome. This is the webpage of the GRiD ("Graduate Researchers in Data") group -- a bunch of people working and approaching data from different angles, such as mathematicians, statisticians, physicists, astronomers, bio-statisticians, computer and social scientists. We are a recognized graduate organization at UMass Amherst and the first group here for graduate students with a focus on data science. You can find out more about us here.
